Scoth eggs

Description

Scotch eggs are made with eggs for a filling and healthy treat

Ingredients

  • 1 quart oil for frying
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 pounds bulk pork sausage
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 4 cups dried bread crumbs, seasoned

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Place eggs in saucepan and cover with water
  3. Flatten sausage and make a patty to surround each egg
  4. Very lightly flour the outer sausage layer then coat with beaten egg
  5. Deep fry prepared eggs in hot oil until golden brown while making sure each
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes
  7. Cut in half and serve
